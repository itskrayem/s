I Love You

Isn't love wonderful?

In your eyes, I found my light,
In your smile, my world feels right.
Your touch is warmth, your words are song,
With you, my heart forever belongs.

Hand in hand, through life we'll go,
Love's gentle breeze, like a river's flow.
In your embrace, I've found my place,
A love so sweet, time can't erase.

Simple joys, shared laughter too,
All I need is me and you.
In every moment, our love will bloom,
Forever together, chasing away the gloom.
